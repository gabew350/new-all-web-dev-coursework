Oatmeal Chocholate Chip Cookies

  • Oats
  • Chocholate
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Baking Soda
  1. Mix the ingredients together
  2. Add Oats and Chocholate Chips
  3. Put them in refrigerator for 45min to prevent overspreding
  4. Scoop cookie dough balls onto the pan
